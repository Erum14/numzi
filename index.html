About the Game

Numbers + Charms = Magic ✨

Numzi blends magnetic number tiles with adorable clay charms so early math feels like pure creative play.

🔢

Number Tiles

Bold magnetic number tiles help children connect written numerals to real quantities — no flashcards needed.

🐞

Clay Charms

Handcrafted clay charms — ladybugs, stars, moons, flowers & more — make sorting irresistibly tactile.

📐

Quantity Matching

Pick a number, place the matching count of charms beside it. Simple concept, deep learning.

📈

Grow Together

Start with 1, 2, 3 — add a new tile as confidence grows. Always at the right challenge level.

How to Play

Four Simple Steps 🎯

No instructions needed — children figure it out naturally through play.

1

Stick Tiles

Stick number tiles on the fridge or any magnetic surface.

2

Mix Charms

Pour the clay charms into a pile and scatter below the tiles.

3

Pick & Match

Child picks a number and places that many charms beside it.

4

Level Up! 🎉

Add tile 4, then 5… always one step ahead of yesterday.
